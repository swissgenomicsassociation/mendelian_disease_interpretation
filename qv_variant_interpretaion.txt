criteria acmg_criteria field=ACMG_criteria operator="not_contains" value="B"

note "ACMG benign evidence codes are retained for review; variants carrying these codes must be justified in the final causal interpretation report"
note "For details on ACMG criteria see Richards et al. 2015, Li et al. 2017, Riggs et al. 2020, Tavtigian et al. 2020"
note "Variants containing benign evidence codes (BA, BS, BP) are not excluded but must be explicitly reviewed in the final report"
